Travelling to Singapore

Getting Here

The best and fastest way to get to Singapore is by air. Singapore’s Changi Airport is one of the busiest airports in the world and consistently the best airport in the world during the pre-pandemic years. It fell to world’s third best airport during the past pandemic years. This is according to a report published by Skytrax World Airport Award in their website www.worldairportawards.com under Winner History section. Changi Airport comprises of four terminals interconnected by Changi Airport Skytrain, and a ten-storey lifestyle hub named Changi Jewel that is also connected to all the airport terminals. Whether you're arriving, departing, or stopping by on a layover, there is something to do for everyone.

Accommodations

Singapore has the best hotels from the most luxurious to the budget friendly hotels and even for backpackers. Travelers who are willing to spend and enjoy are recommended to book their hotel room in Singapore’s iconic hotels mainly the Marina Bay Sands, The Fullerton Hotel Singapore and Raffles Hotel Singapore. Marina Bay Sands comprises three 55-storey towers of extravagant hotel rooms and luxury suites and is home to the world’s largest Infinity Pool glazing down on the glittering expanse of the city’s business district from 57 storeys above. The Fullerton Hotel Singapore is a 5-Star grand neoclassical style hotel built in 1928 with massive classical-styled or Palladian structures. This hotel stands as the nation’s greatest cultural landmark. Raffles Hotel Singapore opened in 1887 is named after Sir Stamford Raffles, it epitomises the island’s colonial history. It once hosted significant figures such as Charlie Chaplin, Rudyard Kipling, Queen Elizabeth II and the King of pop Michael Jackson. For budget friendly but still elegant hotels, travellers can stay in Singapore’s family friendly hotels such as Mandarin Oriental, Parkroyal Hotel Chains, Orchard hotel, The Westin and Accor Chain of Hotels. For the budget-tight individuals there are lots of backpacker hotels to choose from with clean and safe shared hotel room environment.

Getting Around

The city state has all the means for the commuter needs. A traveler can tour the city by train, bus and taxi and available 24x7 and 7 days a week. Having your own vehicle is a luxury in this city since all destinations are reachable by commuting. You can take a cab and go around the whole city east to west, north to south and will not take you more than half a day. Aside from commuting, a traveler can safely tour the city by walking or biking at any time of the day. Pedestrians have a great use of its pedestrian friendly infrastructure of well-lit roads, covered sidewalks, street signs in English and foot bridges decorated with plants and flowers some accessible by elevator.
