Visitor Reviews
 

"Fantastic Place to Visit!" This is the place to go if you want to see a society where respect still exists. Singapore is one of the cleanest and safest countries we have ever visited. Bret Simmons, Canada

“A week in Singapore...” I would return happily for more business trips. Singapore is beautiful, because everything that could detract from it's beauty carries a penalty. People are warm, friendly and very proud of their little oasis in this part of the world. Julie Forster, Massachusets USA

“What's not to love?!?” Singapore is a wonderful, first-world oasis in Southeast Asia. It's an adventurers paradise. If you're looking to see Southeast Asia but still want the first-world comforts of home, with great shopping and dining, fancy cars and coffee shops... Singapore is just the place! Aelwyn Pierre, Netherlands

“A country in a garden!” I've been there a few times. The country is very clean. Travelling around is easy and the MRT provides extensive network. Jennifer Lim, Malaysia
